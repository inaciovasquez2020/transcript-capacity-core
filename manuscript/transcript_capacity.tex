\section*{Transcript Capacity Lower Bound}

Let $(T_t)$ be a transcript process satisfying:

\[
I(W;T_{t+1}\mid T_t) \le b(n).
\]

Then:

\[
I(W;T_T) \le T \cdot b(n).
\]

If correctness requires $I(W;T_T) \ge \gamma H(W)$, then:

\[
T \ge \frac{\gamma H(W)}{b(n)}.
\]
